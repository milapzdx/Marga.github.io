our story

A note from the founders

```

We're writing this from Rishikesh, sitting with teachers who have spent decades preserving traditions that most of the world has never heard of.

My partner has practised Hindu and Buddhist spiritual arts his whole life. I'm a visual artist and designer. Together, we kept asking the same question: why is it so hard to find these teachers if you don't already know where to look?

We've met yoga masters, Ayurvedic healers, Rajasthani painting teachers, sound healers, Vedic chanters — extraordinary people whose knowledge is passed student to student, with no way for the wider world to discover them. That felt wrong to us.

Marga — the path — is what we're building to fix that. A home for authentic spiritual arts and wellness, where students across the UK, Europe, India and beyond can find their teacher, book their session, and begin their path.

We're building this in the open. Follow the journey, join the waitlist, and help us shape what Marga becomes.

With gratitude, from Delhi & Rishikesh 🙏
